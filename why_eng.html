Anais Hernandez
Aerospace Engineering Personally, engineering was not something that I knew I was going to pursue since I was a kid. As a matter of fact, I didn’t want to be one thing, I wanted to pursue about 4 different unrelated careers. When applying to colleges I had a conversation with my cousin who was pursuing a degree in civil engineering and he asked what major I was going to apply to schools as. I was completely split between mathematics and their different branches, but after telling him my interests in math and science, he suggested me to look into engineering. That's when it all clicked, I can combine different subjects I love and still get to apply it in real life! I looked into the branches of engineering and decided that Aerospace was the most compelling. My decision was rather spontaneous.
