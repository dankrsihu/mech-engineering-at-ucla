Corporations and Mechanical Engineering

No matter where you look, there is a job that only a mechanical engineer can do. Here are just a few of the companies looking to hire mechanical engineers.

Description

An independent agency of the United States Federal Government responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and aerospace research.

Values

Safety, Integrity, Teamwork, Excellence.

Mission Statement

Drive advances in science, technology, aeronautics, and space exploration to enhance knowledge, education, innovation, economic vitality and stewardship of Earth

Type of Work

Design engineering and drafting. Design data management and control. Maintaining hardware standards, testing standards, and design processes for flight hardware. Computer-aided engineering solutions for mechanical design and analysis.

What They Look For

Collaboration, Curiosity, Resilience, Flexibility

For More Information

Visit https://www.nasa.gov/careers

Description

An American multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising.

Values

Protecting users. Expanding opportunity. Including all voices. Responding to crises. Advancing sustainability.

Mission Statement

Committed to significantly improving the lives of as many people as possible.

Type of Work

Participate in product design, analysis, and prototyping. Collaborate with vendors and outside sources in order to see parts through to manufacture. Partner with cross-functional teams focusing on challenges related to Product Design. Hardware System Integration.

What They Look For

Collaboration. Knowledge of Coding Languages. Mastery of Mechanical Engineering.

For More Information

Visit https://careers.google.com/teams/engineering-technology/

Description

An American multinational automaker that has its main headquarters in Dearborn, Michigan. The company sells automobiles and commercial vehicles under the Ford brand and most luxury cars under the Lincoln brand.

Values

Put People First. Do the Right Thing. Create Tomorrow. Be Curious. Built Ford Tough.

Mission Statement

To become the world’s most trusted company, designing smart vehicles for a smart world.

Type of Work

Calculate stack-up of detail components. Determine critical dimensions, clearances, and tolerance. Check manufacturing drawings to determine proper fit, finish, function and correctness information. Provide quality CAD for CAE engineers to mesh.

What They Look For

Collaboration. Logical Decision Making. Open Mindset.

For More Information

Visit https://corporate.ford.com/careers.html

Description

An American multinational corporation founded in 1886 that develops medical devices, pharmaceutical, and consumer packaged goods.

Values

Growth and Innovation. Investing in Our Future. Global Diversity, Citizenship, and Sustainability. Develop Our Diverse, Global Supply Base.

Mission Statement

Our Credo Stems From a Belief That Consumers, Employees and the Community Are All Equally Important.

Type of Work

Design and prototype activities including CAD, manufacturing, assembly, testing, debugging, and documentation for mechanical fixtures, Coordinate with different teams and coworkers to accomplish tasks, Generate and present design concepts, perform design reviews to team members.

What They Look For

Data Analysis, Collaboration, Communication, Strategic Thinking.
